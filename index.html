About

Yishuang (Sherry) Xu

I work at the intersection of AI, sustainability, and real estate — as a researcher, author, and founder. My academic work focuses on how emerging technologies reshape real estate investment and the built environment. My practical work turns that research into tools that professionals can actually use.

At the University of Manchester, where I am an Associate Professor, I research PropTech, digital assets, and ESG in real estate. As founder of Plinthos, I build ESG compliance software for UK real estate funds. My writing spans academic journals, practical guides, and self-published books read by analysts and investors worldwide.

My most recent book, PropTech Innovations in Real Estate Investment and Finance: Sustainable Futures (Routledge, 2026), explores how technology is reshaping real estate investment across global markets.

For advisory or collaboration enquiries, get in touch.
