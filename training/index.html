Training & workshops

Practitioner training on AI, ESG, and tokenisation in real estate

Half-day and full-day workshops for property firms, fund managers, and professional bodies — delivered by a researcher who also builds the tools. Theory grounded in peer-reviewed research; exercises grounded in real deals and real data.

Workshop topics

AI

AI for Real Estate Professionals

How to evaluate, implement, and govern AI tools in property investment and management — from automated valuations and portfolio analytics to the RICS mandatory standard on responsible AI use.

Covers: AI readiness assessment for your firm · prompt engineering for IC memos, AM reports, and ESG analysis · evaluating third-party AI tools · RICS AI standard compliance · building an internal AI governance policy
ESG

ESG Compliance for UK Real Estate

Navigating the reporting landscape from MEES and EPC compliance through Scope 3 measurement to UK SRS and GRESB submission — with practical exercises using real portfolio data.

Covers: MEES 2030 deadlines and cost caps · Scope 3 data hierarchy and estimation methods · UK SRS disclosure requirements · GRESB scoring optimisation · building an ESG data collection framework · assurance readiness
Tokenisation

Real Estate Tokenisation: From Concept to Structure

How real estate tokenisation works in practice — the legal structures, regulatory requirements, and due diligence frameworks that separate viable projects from white papers.

Covers: How tokenisation works (SPVs, smart contracts, custody) · UK regulatory landscape and FCA considerations · deal evaluation and structuring · fractional ownership models · investor communications · secondary market considerations
